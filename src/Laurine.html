How to Make Delicious Lasagna Bolognese?

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • Lasagna noodles
  • 24 oz. bolognese sauce
  • 1 lbs. ground beef
  • 4 oz. shredded Swiss cheese or
    grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large onion

Bechamel Sauce

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 table spoons flour
  • 5 oz. butter

Directions

 

Step 1

Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.

 

Step 2

While lasagna noodles are cooking, heat large skillet over medium high heat. Slice and cook the onion. Add and cook ground beef. Season with salt and pepper. When meat is cooked, pour bolognese sauce. Stir occasionally.

 

Step 3

Start preparing the bechamel sauce in a saucepan: melt butter and then add flour. Stir until well combined. Add milk while stirring. Keep stirring the ingredients until the bechamel sauce gets thicker.

 

Step 4

Mix bolognese sauce with bechamel sauce.

 

Step 5

Drain noodles.

 

Step 6

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

 

Step 7

In a baking dish, arrange noodles lengthwise and add the sauce between each layer.

 

Step 8

Top with a final layer of noodles and sprinkle with cheese.

 

Step 9

Bake for 45 minutes. Serve and....

 

Buon Appetito!
